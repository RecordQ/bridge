# **App Name**: Bridge Ltd

## Core Features:

- Animated Hero Section: Home page with a captivating space-themed animation to immediately grab attention, using 3js for 3d parallax effects.
- Product Showcase: Product showcase: high-quality images of customizable products (USBs, gift boxes, pens, etc.) with space-themed parallax effects.
- Simple Pricing Page: Pricing page that displays items and prices (no checkout functionality) with subtle space animations.
- AI Chatbot: AI-powered chat tool that gives answers using gemini for questions. The chat tool has context about your company and keeps conversation history.
- Firebase CMS: Firebase integration to manage pricing and content dynamically.
- Admin Authentication: Authentication for Admin Panel using a simple username and password form.
- Google Drive Integration: Integration of Google Drive for uploading and storing images to use on the price page, and elsewhere as needed.
- About Page: An informative page detailing the company's mission, values, and history, enhanced with space-themed parallax.
- Contact Page: A page to allow users to easily contact the company through forms, emails, or other contact information, with subtle space animations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep space purple (#4A148C) to evoke the vastness and mystery of space.
- Background color: Dark charcoal gray (#212121), near black, providing a clean and modern backdrop for the space visuals and content.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF), a vibrant and energetic counterpoint to the deep purple. This accent color highlights interactive elements.
- Supplemental color: Soft pink (#F48FB1) to add a touch of warmth and creativity.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headings to emphasize the futuristic tech-based feeling, and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body text for clear and accessible information.
- Use custom-designed icons with a line-art style, incorporating subtle gradients and animations on hover to maintain a futuristic and dynamic feel. Icons should relate to navigation or indicate a particular feature of your service.
- A clean, modern design with generous spacing to allow the stunning space visuals and product images to breathe. Employ a grid-based system for responsive design.
- Subtle but impactful space animations and motion graphics throughout the site. Space-themed motion graphics with parallax scrolling effects, and 3D elements using 3js.
